Our Story

A living
heritage of stone

Rajasthan Marble Arts was born from a simple, profound belief: that the finest marble in the world deserves the finest hands in the world. Located in Alwar — a city whose dust carries centuries of stone-craft — our studio has been the silent architect behind sacred temples, palatial homes, and landmark hotel lobbies.

"Every chisel mark is a conversation between the artisan and the stone. We do not impose — we reveal."

Under the vision of CEO Himanshu Mishra, we have expanded from local commissions to global operations — exporting to Dubai, managing large-scale architectural programmes, and integrating 3D modelling and AI design tools into an ancient craft. The tradition remains. The reach has grown.

Himanshu Mishra is more than a businessman — he is a custodian of a living art form. Raised among the marble workshops of Alwar, his understanding of stone is intuitive, almost spiritual. He has spent decades bridging the world of traditional Rajasthani craftsmanship with the expectations of global luxury clientele.

We do not sell marble. We create heirlooms — objects that outlive us and carry the soul of Rajasthan into every corner of the world.
